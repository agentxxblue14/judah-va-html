# Vendor Vetting Scorecard
**Sample built by Judah for the Lifestyle Manager role. This is the standard I run before any contractor, landscaper, or service gets hired for either home.** A live, clickable version is built into the pitch site.

The point: vetting a vendor should not be a gut feeling. It's a short, repeatable check so the right person gets hired and you never wonder why one slipped through.

## The 6 checks (each worth points)
| # | Criterion | Why it matters | Points if yes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Licensed & insured (verified, not claimed) | Protects the home and you from liability | 25 |
| 2 | Strong recent reviews (4.5+, last 6 months) | Pattern of real, current quality | 20 |
| 3 | Written quote in budget range | No surprise invoices | 20 |
| 4 | Responsive (replies within 1 business day) | Predicts how they'll behave once hired | 15 |
| 5 | Provides 2+ references / past clients | Verifiable track record | 10 |
| 6 | Clear on scope, timeline, and cleanup | Professional, not improvising | 10 |
| | **Total possible** | | **100** |

## Verdict bands
- **80 to 100 — GO.** Book it. Confirm date, scope, and price in writing, add to the command center, set the reminder.
- **55 to 79 — HOLD.** One or two gaps. Get the missing piece (proof of insurance, a reference, a firm quote) before committing. Keep a backup quote warm.
- **Below 55 — PASS.** Too many unknowns. Source 2 more options. A cheap quote with no insurance is not a deal.

## Worked example (illustrative)
**"Driveway repair, Home B, two quotes in."**

Vendor A — licensed/insured (verified) ✓, reviews 4.7 recent ✓, quote $2,950 (in range) ✓, replied same day ✓, gave 3 references ✓, scope a bit vague on cleanup ✗.
Score: 25 + 20 + 20 + 15 + 10 + 0 = **90 → GO.** Action: confirm cleanup in writing, then book.

Vendor B — license "in progress" ✗, reviews 4.2 ✓-ish (counted as no, under threshold) ✗, quote $2,100 ✓, slow to reply (3 days) ✗, no references ✗, clear scope ✓.
Score: 0 + 0 + 20 + 0 + 0 + 10 = **30 → PASS.** Cheaper, but uninsured and unproven. Not worth the risk on the home.

Recommendation to you: **Book Vendor A** once the cleanup line is added. I'd handle that exchange and have it on the calendar same day.

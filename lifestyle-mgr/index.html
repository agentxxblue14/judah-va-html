What I'd build in the first two weeks

Week 1

Intake and command center live

A short call to learn both homes, your vendors, and your preferences. Then the command center goes live with everything you already have loaded in, so nothing lives in your head anymore.

Week 1

Vendor list vetted

Your current providers run through the scorecard, gaps flagged, and the "to source" list started for anything missing. You see exactly who's solid and who needs a backup.

Week 2

Recurring stuff on autopilot

Deliveries, filters, and subscriptions mapped and set to recur. Returns and open orders cleaned up. The routine work stops needing a human.

Week 2

The Friday digest starts

You get one two-minute screen each week: what got done, what's pending, and the few decisions only you can make. That's the load, lifted.

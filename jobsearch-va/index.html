The system you are hiring

A six-step weekly loop, every week

This is what turns "apply to a lot of jobs" into a calm, repeatable pipeline. Nothing slips, because every step has a home.

01 · SOURCE

Find the roles

Sweep LinkedIn, SEEK, Indeed and named company career pages for remote roles matched to each of you.

02 · SCORE

Filter for fit

Run each role through a match scorecard so we apply to the right ones with real effort, not spray-and-pray.

03 · TAILOR

Customise everything

Adapt the resume and cover letter to each posting and to ATS keywords, in your voice, AI-drafted and human-reviewed.

04 · SUBMIT

Apply cleanly

05 · TRACK

Log every application

06 · FOLLOW-UP

Chase and report

Send follow-ups on schedule, watch both inboxes for interviews, book them in your calendars, and send a Friday recap.

In my own words

A system I made better.

A client's inbox was the bottleneck. Messages slipped, replies were slow, and nothing was tracked. I mapped the recurring message types, then built an email triage workflow that auto-sorts and summarises incoming mail and flags only what needs a human decision. I documented it as a simple SOP so it runs without me babysitting it. The result: a clean, prioritised inbox every morning, faster replies, and hours of manual sorting gone each week. I now reuse that same system across multiple client accounts.

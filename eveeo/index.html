Responsibility 2 · Scope teardown

From a one-line request to an executable, technical scope

A "should we build chargers here" or "stand up an inspection lane" request hides dozens of requirements. I break it into buckets, flag bottlenecks early, and hand the technical team a checklist instead of a vibe. Here are the two domains in your post.

EV charging deployment

What the scope has to nail

  • Electrical load demand and transformer upgrade needs
  • Utility interconnection and permitting timeline
  • Level 2 vs DC Fast Charging hardware selection
  • Hardware and software interoperability
  • ADA compliance and physical site layout
  • Supply chain lead times flagged as schedule risk
Vehicle inspection setup

What the scope has to nail

  • Diagnostic tool integration and calibration steps
  • Physical lane layout and throughput planning
  • Safety testing and regulatory compliance criteria
  • Data management and records systems
  • Technician training and quality-control checks
  • Permitting and regional compliance factors

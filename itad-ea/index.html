Application requirement #4

How I make sure important emails are never missed

I treat the inbox like a system, not a pile. Scheduled sweeps, four buckets, nothing archived until it is done or logged.

01 · SWEEP

At set times

I process the inbox at scheduled points in the day instead of reacting all day long.

02 · SORT

Four buckets

Every email becomes Urgent, Action, Waiting, or FYI. No email sits unsorted.

03 · FLAG

Urgent to you

Urgent items go to you right away with a one-line summary and the decision needed.

04 · LOG

Never on memory

Anything actionable becomes a task with an owner and due date, logged in the triage tab. Inbox ends at zero.

How this works

Add leads, set a fit score, and the system schedules each follow-up for you. Anything due today or overdue turns red up top and in the "Due now" filter. Change a status and the next due date recalculates on the cadence. Hit "Weekly report" for the one-block summary to send the CEO. Everything saves in this browser, and Export CSV gives you the file for Sheets or a CRM.
Sample working system built for Jamie LeClaire's EA / Operations Manager role by Judah Rivera. Seed data is illustrative. Your data stays in this browser (localStorage); nothing is uploaded anywhere.

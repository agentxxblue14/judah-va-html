You give me the criteria (industry, size, region, title to reach). I build the list, keep it clean in the CRM, run a short outreach cadence, and report back what actually moved. No vanity numbers, just "here is who replied and what they said."

## Outreach cadence (3 touches, then nurture)
**Touch 1 (day 0)** — short, specific to them, one ask.
> Subject: quick one about [their thing]
> Hi [First name], saw [specific, real observation about their company]. We help [people like them] with [the one outcome], without [the annoying part]. Worth a quick look? Happy to send a 2-minute example, no call needed unless you want one.

**Touch 2 (day 4, if no reply)** — add value, do not nag.
> Following up with the example I mentioned: [link]. If lead gen/ops support isn't a priority right now, totally fine, just let me know and I'll close the loop.

**Touch 3 (day 9, breakup)** — easy yes/no.
> Last note from me on this. If it's a no for now I'll stop here. If it's a "not yet," say the word and I'll check back in [month].

After touch 3 with no reply: move to Nurture, revisit in 60 days. I never let a lead sit in limbo.

## What I report to you (weekly, one block)
- Leads added: X (criteria-matched)
- Replies: Y · Calls booked: Z
- Top 3 warm ones and what they need next
- Anything I learned about the messaging that is or isn't landing

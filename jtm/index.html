The system, not just the tasks

How I would run your week

You said you want someone who builds the system, not someone who waits to be told. Here is what that looks like beyond one-off requests.

Schedule, kept ahead of you

  • Calendar organized, focus time blocked and protected
  • Conflicts spotted and rescheduled before they collide
  • Commitments surfaced before you would have forgotten them
  • Briefing notes for meetings: who, where, parking, a backup venue

Operations, quietly tracked

  • A deadline tracker: passport renewals, business filings, subscription reviews
  • Contact lists maintained and tidy
  • Personal admin and email handled with discretion
  • Repetitive requests turned into a repeatable system, then automated

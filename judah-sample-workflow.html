The system behind it

The SOP, step by step.

No guesswork, no babysitting. This runs the same way every morning, and it's written down so it survives holidays, sick days, and scale.

01

Sweep every channel

First thing in your morning, I pull everything new from your email and LinkedIn DMs into one view. Nothing lives in two places. Nothing gets missed.

GmailLinkedInShared inbox
02

Sort into three buckets

Each message gets read and tagged: Flag if it needs your decision, Handle if it's routine and I can action it from your SOPs, or Archive if it needs nothing. The judgment call is the whole job, and it's the part I take seriously.

Your SOPsLabels / tags
03

Handle the routine, on-brand

Welcomes, replay links, FAQ replies, refund-policy answers: I action these using your saved wording so every member hears your voice, not mine. Supportive vibes only.

Saved repliesCommunity tags
04

Draft what you'll likely need

For flagged items, I prep a draft reply where it's obvious, so saying yes is one click for you, not a blank page. You stay in control; I remove the friction.

Draft repliesCalendar checks
05

Send one brief, before your day

Everything lands as a single short brief: what needs you, what I handled, what I ignored. You read it in two minutes and get on with coaching.

Daily briefAction points
06

Spot the patterns, improve the system

When the same question keeps coming up, I flag it and propose a new saved reply or a small fix. The workflow gets smarter over time, so your inbox keeps shrinking, not growing.

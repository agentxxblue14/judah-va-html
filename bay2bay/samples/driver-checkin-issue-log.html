This shows how I would run driver communication and keep the records you asked for. The SOP keeps it consistent and professional with proper boundaries; the log makes sure nothing falls through the cracks. Names and entries are illustrative.

Daily check-in SOP

  1. Morning ping (group chat): post the day's reminders, confirm each driver is rolling, note anyone who does not respond by the cutoff.
  2. Listen and triage: answer routine questions directly. Anything about pay, safety, or a vehicle problem gets logged immediately.
  3. Log it: every real concern goes in the issue log with date, driver, category, and next step. Nothing lives only in chat.
  4. Resolve or escalate: handle what I can within policy. Escalate to ownership only for major decisions or emergencies, with a one-line summary and a recommendation.
  5. Close the loop: follow up with the driver, mark resolved, keep the record.

Boundaries: friendly and respectful, never personal. Consistent rules for everyone. Promises only on what policy allows.

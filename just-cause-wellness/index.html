Sample 1 · the booked-call engine

Who to reach, and the messages that get a call

"Follow corporate wellness leads and book discovery calls" is the line in your post that grows the business. So I start with who actually says yes to a workplace wellbeing chat, then warm them with messages that sound like a person, not a pitch deck.

Who I'd target
Decision maker
People & Culture
HR Directors, People & Culture leads, Heads of People. They own staff wellbeing, retention and the perks budget. A self-care program answers burnout and turnover directly, which is what keeps them up at night.
Day-to-day buyer
Office & workplace
Office Managers, Workplace Experience leads, EAs to founders. They plan the team days and the care packages. Lower friction, faster yes, often the warmest first contact.
Owner / budget
Founders & COOs
Founders and COOs of 50 to 300 person companies. Small enough to decide quickly, big enough to have a wellbeing line item. Good when they post about culture or hiring fast.

Signals I'd watch for: posts about burnout or return-to-office, "great place to work" awards, fast hiring, new HR hires. Each is a natural reason to reach out.

Ends on an easy question. Frames us as removing work from HR, not adding a project.

No pressure either way. We put together a short [free team self-care checklist / 5-minute reset guide] that a few people teams have found handy, want me to send it over? Even if we never work together it's yours.
A genuine free resource earns the reply and positions us as helpful, not hungry.
Day 7The askbook the discovery call
If looking after the team is on your list this quarter, I'd love 15 minutes to hear what you're after and show what we've done for similar teams. Here's my link: [Calendly]. If the timing's off, just say and I'll check back later instead of nudging.
Direct CTA with a booking link. The "I'll check back, not nudge" line lowers resistance.
Day 14Soft closeleave the door open
Last note from me, [First name]. I'll leave the checklist with you and stay connected. If wellbeing moves up the list later, you know where I am. Either way, glad we connected.
Graceful exit. Keeps the relationship warm for when budget or timing changes.
